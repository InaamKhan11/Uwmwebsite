FAQs

  • What is the hazardous waste?

    Any waste that have a potential to damage the environment or it can be potentially harmful to humans or animals. For example, Electric Generator oil, Ewaste, Broken Glass, Harmful chemicals, Hospital waste, etc.

  • What can be called as non-Hazardous waste?

    As the name implies, it is a type of waste that is not harmful to the environment or to any life. It includes, kitchen waste, packaging materials, etc

  • Do you have a valid license for waste handling?

    Handling of waste not only requires license from the authorities but it also requires proper handling expertise. UWM is proud to announce that we have valid NOC from Sindh Environmental Protection Agency (SEPA) and Sindh Solid Waste Management Board (SSWMB). We hold decades of experience in this field.
